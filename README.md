# 💻 Mini Project 1 – Choosing a Computer on a Budget (€1000)

## 📘 Project Overview

This mini project focuses on recommending the best laptop for a college student with a limited budget. The goal was to analyze several laptop options and select the most suitable device based on performance, portability, battery life, reliability, longevity, and price.

The project simulates a real-world scenario where an IT support specialist helps a customer choose the best computer according to their technical and financial requirements.

---

# 📖 Scenario

Meet John, a college student with a tight budget of **€1000**. His old laptop has become too slow for programming, development environments, and virtual machines required for his computer science coursework.

John requires a laptop that offers:

* ✅ Strong performance for coding and virtual machines
* ✅ Lightweight and portable design
* ✅ Good battery life
* ✅ Reliability and customer support
* ✅ Longevity (3–4 years minimum)
* ✅ Best value within budget

# 🎯 Why Lenovo ThinkPad E16 Gen 2 Was Selected

The Lenovo ThinkPad E16 Gen 2 was selected because it provides the best balance between performance, portability, reliability, and price for a computer science student.

The AMD Ryzen 7 processor combined with 32 GB DDR5 RAM ensures smooth performance when running development environments, programming tools, and virtual machines. The laptop also offers excellent multitasking capabilities without freezing or slowing down.

Additionally, the ThinkPad series is well known for its durability, business-class reliability, and strong keyboard quality. The device weighs approximately 1.81 kg, making it portable enough for a student who frequently moves between classes and study locations.

Battery life of up to 15 hours also makes it suitable for long study sessions without frequent charging.

The strong hardware configuration ensures that the laptop will remain useful and capable for several years, satisfying the longevity requirement.

# ❌ Why the Other Laptops Were Not Selected

## Asus Vivobook S 15 OLED

* Strong specifications and excellent display
* Better suited for multimedia usage
* Not as durable or business-focused as the ThinkPad series
* Reliability and long-term support may not match Lenovo ThinkPad

## Lenovo ThinkPad P15 Gen 2

* Very powerful workstation-class laptop
* Heavy (~2.7 kg)
* Lower portability
* Shorter battery life
* Refurbished model

## Dell Precision 7550 (Refurbished)

* Older generation hardware
* Heavy (~2.5 kg)
* Less portable
* Older processor architecture
* Refurbished condition reduces long-term value
